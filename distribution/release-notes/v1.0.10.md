## Initial release

Essential Key Tools makes the Nothing Phone Essential Key work the way you want, without root or a computer.

- Assign separate shortcuts to single, double, triple, and long presses.
- Open an app, toggle the flashlight, take a screenshot, lock the screen, control media, or change the ringer mode.
- Follow a guided setup that clearly explains the optional accessibility service before Android settings open.
- Test the Essential Key without triggering the shortcut assigned to it.
- Use the app in English or Traditional Chinese, with wording tailored for users in Taiwan.
- Check for updates manually through Google Play or GitHub, depending on where the app was installed.
